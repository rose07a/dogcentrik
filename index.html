When Dogcentrik watches your pet

There are numerous activities for dogs here. We toss a tennis ball or frisbee for dogs who are into that. Also, we have a kiddy pool for dogs to cool off during the warm months. If your four-legged friend likes car rides, those can happen occasionally too. As long as you and your dog(s) are ok with it, a dog park visit is a possibility. (We have a minivan, so your dog will stay dry and comfortable during transport to and from the dog park). With most dogs, an exhausted dog is a relaxed, happy dog, so we tend to focus on having the dogs play with each other as well as me, in our amazingly landscaped big back yard. For cold, wet days we have a covered back porch and heated area so that dogs can stay dry and warm. Of course, they still have access to the back yard if they feel like getting into a bit of muddy fun too!
