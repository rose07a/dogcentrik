About dogcentrik

Hi there fellow dog lover! At Dogcentrik we are very enthusiastic about dogs and their care. At Dogcentrik we frequently accept late night (up to 8pm) and last-minute bookings as well. We really look forward to meeting you and your furry friends!

Why choose Dogcentrik?

  • COVID-19 update:

    Online payments, contactless pick up, and contactless drop off offered!

  • Dog walks

    Dogs are walked throughout the week (certain conditions apply) at no extra charge. Walks usually range from 25-45 minutes.

  • Dog treats

    At Dogcentrik dogs are given free, homemade, all-natural dog treats each week!

  • Flexibility with scheduling

    Other dog daycares have only a small time frame for drop-off and pick up.

  • Updates

    Daily photo and video updates are provided free of charge. Some other dog daycares charge money for this add-on, or don't offer it.

  • Reliability

    We are open Mon-Sat. (Closed Sundays and the following holidays: Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, Thanksgiving Day, and New Year's Day.)

When Dogcentrik watches your pet

There are numerous activities for dogs here. We toss a tennis ball or frisbee for dogs who are into that. Also, we have a kiddy pool for dogs to cool off during the warm months. If your four-legged friend likes car rides, those can happen occasionally too. As long as you and your dog(s) are ok with it, a dog park visit is a possibility. (We have a minivan, so your dog will stay dry and comfortable during transport to and from the dog park). With most dogs, an exhausted dog is a relaxed, happy dog, so we tend to focus on having the dogs play with each other as well as me, in our amazingly landscaped big back yard. For cold, wet days we have a covered back porch and heated area so that dogs can stay dry and warm. Of course, they still have access to the back yard if they feel like getting into a bit of muddy fun too!
