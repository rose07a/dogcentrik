Daycare Policies

  • Bookings

    Please make your booking as soon as you possibly can. We do offer last minute bookings. However, the slot is not guaranteed. To book you may call, text, or email Dogcentrik. Many clients text us. Example: "Hi! I need daycare for my dog on the following days, Mon 10/7 and Weds 10/9 with both days being from 9am-6pm, do u have availability on those days and times?"

  • Drop Off

    Please call or text 541-654-2367 before arrival. This is for your convenience, so that we can adequately prepare to receive your dog. Please bring a leash when dropping off. This is for dog safety and owner's convenience. Thank you!

  • Pick Up

    Please text us even if there is already a pre-agreed upon time. Pickup is available after 6:30pm for an extra $10. we do not offer pickup after 9:00pm.

  • Meals

    We offer a breakfast and a dinner feeding with owner-provided food. During meal times, dogs are put in an individual area with no other dogs, so as to ensure a smooth and calm eating experience. For first time clients who would like their dog to eat here we ask that you bring high-quality canned dog food or chicken broth to entice your pooch to eat, as they may be not hungry in a new and lively environment. Please bring any food or treats you'd like your dog to have to have and let us know if they have any food allergies or medication needs.

  • Leash

    Unless other arrangements have been made, we kindly ask that all owners keep their dogs leashed at all times. This is both for dog safety and owner convenience. What we've seen work best is to leash the dog well ahead of drop-off, before they ever get in the vehicle. We also kindly ask that all leashes are fixed-length, and not the stretchy, adjustable kind as we view them to be unsafe. Thank you greatly for abiding by this policy.

  • Photos/updates

    We reserve the right to store, use, and distribute photos and videos of your dog(s), in perpetuity.

  • Pricing/discounts:

    Discounts and pricing are at the sole discretion of Dogcentrik. We do offer a $10 discount for each additional dog you bring into the pack.

  • Vaccine

    Dogs must be current on the following vaccines: Rabis, DHPP(Parvo), Bordetella(Kennel Cough). Please be able to provide proof of vaccines via email or in-person with paper copies.

  • Breed Restrictions:

    We do not offer care for German Shepherds or German Shepherd mixes. We apologize for any inconvenience.

FAQs

  • 1. Does Dogcentrik offer overnight boarding?

    At this time we only offer high quality daycare.

  • 2. When is payment for services due?

    Payment is due in full, before or upon pick up.

  • 3. Is a meet n greet required?

    While not technically required, whenever possible a meet n greet at our location is highly recommended to see if it's a good fit for both parties.
